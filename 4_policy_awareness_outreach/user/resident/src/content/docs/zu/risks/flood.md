title: Ingozi Yokuhlwithwa
description: Ingozi Yokuhlwithwa
---

Ukuhlanjululwa kuyingozi yokufukamela emzantsi nasezintshonalanga onke amazwe onke. Ukuhlanjululwa kuvamile ukwenzeka uma imvula eqhubekayo iphalala ngezinsuku ezingaphezu kwezimbili, uma imvula enamandla yehlisela ngenhla ngesikhathi esifushane, noma lapho umjikelezo wokuqothulwa kweqhwa noma udoti udala ukuba umfula noma umjikelezo uwawushise emhlabeni ozungezile. Ukuhlanjululwa kungavela futhi ngenxa yokwehluleka kwesakhiwo sokulawula amanzi, njenge-levee noma idamu. Imbangela evamile yokuhlanjululwa yamanzi anokwenzeka ngenxa yemvula aqhubeka ngokushesha kunokuthi umhlabathi akwazi ukukwazi noma imifula ikwazi ukuyithwala. Cishe amashumi ayisikhombisa n Five percent (75%) wezinkalankala zomthelela ogama nqobo ziphakathi kokuhlanjululwa. Ngezansi kukhona izingozi ezivamile zokuhlanjululwa.
